Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

The Notevs app places great importance on user privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share user data with third parties.

Data Collected

Our app does not collect any personal data. All notes entered by the user are stored only on the user's own device and are not transferred outside the app.

Use of Data

Data entered by the user is only displayed within the app and is entirely under the user's control. This data is not sent to the developer or any server.

Data Sharing

The app does not share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically targeted at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from this age group.

Security

Since all data is stored only on the user's device, data security depends entirely on the security of the device.

Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.
